Helm Guided Solutions

A high level of precision and accuracy

CHROME GuidedSMILE brings digital, full-arch guided reconstruction to your implant cases, and Helm was the first exclusive provider of the system in Texas.

The protocol anchors every stage to a single fixed reference. That anchor governs how the bite is verified, how the bone is reduced, how each site is drilled, and how the provisional is seated — then it carries all surgical and restorative information forward into the final conversion phase.

Beyond CHROME, we run coDiagnostiX and Dentsply Sirona Simplant, so we can match the guided system to the case in front of you rather than the other way around.

One reference, held throughout

Why anchoring changes the result

With every stage tied back to one unmoving anchor, the small errors that usually compound between steps have nowhere to gather.

Verified, Not Assumed

The bite is confirmed against the anchor before any bone is touched, so the plan you approved is the plan that proceeds.

Cut To A Plane

Bone reduction follows a pre-set plane rather than a freehand judgement, giving consistent prosthetic space across the arch.

Placed, Then Restored

Drilling and provisionalization both reference the same base, so the temporary seats where the design said it would.
